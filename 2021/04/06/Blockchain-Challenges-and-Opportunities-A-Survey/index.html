Blockchain Challenges and Opportunities: A Survey

challenges:

  • scalability:every ten minutes 1 block.the Bitcoin network is restricted to a rate of 7 transactions per second, which is incapable of dealing with high frequency trading. However, larger blocks means larger storage space and slower propagation in the network
  • privacy leakage can also happen in blockchain even when users only make transactions with their public key and private key . User’s real IP address can even be tracked .
our paper focuses on state-of-art blockchain researches including recent advances and future trends

  1. blockchain architecture

    block header:

    1. Block version
    2. Parent block hash
    3. Merkle tree root hash
    4. Timestamp
    5. nBits: current hashing target in compact format.
    6. Nonce

    block body: a transaction counter and transactions.

    Current blockchain systems can be roughly categorized into three types:

    blockchain, consortium blockchain and private blockchain

    key characteristics

    Decentralization Persistency Anonymity Auditability

  2. typical consensus algorithms

    PoW PoS PBFT DPoS

    Ripple :If the received agreements have reached 80%,the transaction will be packed into the ledger. For a node, the ledger will remain correct as long as the percentage of faulty nodes in UNL is less than 20%.

    Tendermint:similar to PBFT,but Tendermint nodes have to lock their coins to become validators. Once a validator is found to be dishonest, it will be punished.
